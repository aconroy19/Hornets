About The NRSA

The North Rockland Soccer Association (NRSA) was founded in1978 by Elvio Nardi, a local barber, and his wife Fran. Their sole intention was to allow the children in North Rockland to have a place to learn and play soccer. Since the Nardis started the NRSA, it has become one of the largest recreational programs in the area. The NRSA serves the North Rockland community for boys and girls of all ages. Along with the recreational program, there is a travel program for children who want to play on a more competitive level.

About The Hornets

The North Rockland Hornets was formed in the Fall of 2017, and they have been one of the NRSA's top competitive teams in recent years. They consist of girls born in the year of 2009. They have several first place performances within the Northern Counties Soccer league. Last year they became elgible to play in a premiere league, and were undefeated their first season. They are continuing to grow and improve, and they represent the NRSA quite well.
